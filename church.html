Who We Are

Rooted in Faith,
Led by the Spirit

Gilgal Dominion Center is a Spirit-filled Pentecostal church committed to preaching the undiluted Word of God. We are a community of believers passionate about worship, discipleship, and extending God's kingdom across Tanzania and beyond.

Our mission is simple: encounter God, grow in faith, and transform our world. From our vibrant Sunday services to midweek Bible studies, every gathering is designed to bring you closer to Christ.
