Our Story

Rooted in Faith,
Led by the Spirit

Gilgal Dominion Center was founded on the simple conviction that every life can be transformed by a genuine encounter with God. For over 14 years we have been a beacon of hope, healing, and holiness in the heart of Dar es Salaam.

We are uncompromisingly Pentecostal — believing in the full gospel, the gifts of the Holy Spirit, and the power of prayer to change every circumstance. Whether you are walking in faith for decades or searching for the very first time, you are welcome here.

The Word

Uncompromising, practical, life-giving Scripture teaching.

Prayer

Taking the Gospel beyond our walls to Tanzania and the world.
