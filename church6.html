Our Story & Vision

About Gilgal Dominion Center

A community rooted in faith, driven by love, and committed to seeing every person discover their God-given purpose.

Our Story

From a Small Gathering to a Growing Kingdom

Gilgal Dominion Center was founded with a singular vision: to create a church where every person, regardless of background, social status, or history, would find a warm welcome and life-changing encounter with Jesus Christ.

The name "Gilgal" carries deep biblical significance — it was the place where the children of Israel rolled away the reproach of Egypt (Joshua 5:9), a place of new beginnings, divine commissioning, and fresh consecration. That is the spirit that drives everything we do at this church.

Today, Gilgal Dominion Center is one of Dodoma's most vibrant faith communities, with ministries spanning children, youth, women, men, evangelism, and community outreach. We believe the church is not a building — it is a living, breathing family of God's people.

🎯

Our Vision

To be a church that transforms Dodoma — and all of Tanzania — through the power of the Gospel.

💡

Our Mission

To make disciples, equip believers, serve communities, and exalt the name of Jesus Christ above all.

📖

Our Values

Faith, Family, Excellence, Integrity, and the Uncompromising Authority of God's Word.

Our Pastors

EP

Ps. Emmanuel Mwanga

Senior Pastor

Senior Pastor & Founder
Ps. Emmanuel Mwanga

Pastor Emmanuel Mwanga holds a Bachelor of Theology from the Africa Inland Church University and has ministered across Tanzania, Kenya, and Uganda. He founded Gilgal Dominion Center over 15 years ago after a powerful divine encounter that commissioned him to build a church for "the forgotten and the overlooked."

Known for his dynamic preaching, prophetic insight, and deep compassion, Pastor Emmanuel is married and has three children. He also serves on the board of the Tanzania Evangelical Council of Churches.

GP

Ps. Grace Mwanga

Associate Pastor

Associate Pastor & Women's Ministry Director
Ps. Grace Mwanga

Pastor Grace is a licensed counselor and ordained minister with a heart for women, families, and children. She holds a Diploma in Christian Counseling and has walked with hundreds of families through healing, restoration, and spiritual growth.

She leads our "Hallelujah Sisters" women's fellowship, oversees Sunday school and children's church, and conducts weekly inner healing sessions. Her gentle authority and prophetic gifting make her a beloved shepherd in the Gilgal family.

You Belong Here

Join Our Church Family

Gilgal Dominion Center is a place where you are known, loved, and needed. Take the first step toward belonging.

The name of our church — Gilgal — means "the reproach has been rolled away." Whatever your past, whatever your story, you are welcome here. We are not a perfect church, but we are a church that loves perfectly because we follow the One who is perfect love.

1

Visit a Sunday Service

The best way to start is simply to come. Sunday services are at 8:00 AM and 10:30 AM. Expect warm greetings, powerful worship, and life-changing teaching.

We run a free monthly "Foundations" class for new attendees — a 4-week journey through faith, church life, and your identity in Christ. No obligations.

3

Get rooted in community through our small groups that meet throughout Dodoma every week. This is where real friendships and growth happen.

Honor God With Your Substance

Give / Toa Sadaka

Your generosity fuels the mission. Every shilling given is a seed sown into God's Kingdom work in Dodoma and beyond.

"Give, and it will be given to you. A good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over, will be poured into your lap."
Luke 6:38

Giving is an act of worship. When you sow into the work of Gilgal Dominion Center, you are partnering with God to transform lives, fund outreaches, support the poor, and carry the Gospel across Tanzania. Thank you for your faithfulness.

🌾

Tithes (Zaka)

10%

The tithe is 10% of your income — the first fruits belonging to God. Bring your tithes to honor the Lord and open the windows of heaven over your life (Malachi 3:10).

💛

Offerings (Sadaka)

Any Amount

A freewill offering from a cheerful heart. God loves a cheerful giver (2 Cor 9:7). Give what you have purposed in your heart, with joy and expectation.
