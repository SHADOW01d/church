Our Story

Rooted in Faith,
Led by the Spirit

Gilgal Dominion Center was founded on the conviction that every life can be transformed by a genuine encounter with God. For over 14 years we have been a beacon of hope, healing, and holiness in the heart of Dar es Salaam.

We are uncompromisingly Pentecostal — believing in the full gospel, the gifts of the Holy Spirit, and the transforming power of prayer.

The Word

Uncompromising, practical, life-giving Scripture teaching every week.

Prayer

A culture of intercession that underpins everything we do together.

Community

Real relationships that carry each other through every season of life.

Mission

Taking the Gospel beyond our walls to Tanzania and the whole world.
