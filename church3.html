Our Story

A Place Where
Every Person Belongs

Gilgal Dominion Center is a vibrant, Spirit-filled church located in the heart of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Founded on the unshakeable Word of God, we are a community where faith is ignited, lives are transformed, and every person finds their place.

Our name speaks of a sacred place of new beginnings. Just as Gilgal was where the reproach of Egypt was rolled away from Israel, we believe this is a place where burdens are lifted, shame is removed, and destinies are unlocked through the power of Jesus Christ.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a long-standing member, you will find a warm, loving family waiting to walk alongside you in your journey of faith.
