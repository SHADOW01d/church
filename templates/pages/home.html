About Us

A Heavenly Place
on Earth

Gilgal Dominion Center is a Spirit-filled, Bible-based church community rooted in the love of God and empowered by the Holy Spirit. We are committed to transforming lives, restoring families, and releasing God's people into their divine destiny.


Founded on the principle that every person — regardless of background, tribe, or station in life — belongs in God's house. We worship, pray, grow, and serve together as one family united in Christ Jesus.

"For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord — plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future." — Jeremiah 29:11
What We Do

Our Ministries

Every ministry arm is designed to meet you where you are and bring you closer to who God created you to be.

Worship & Arts

Our worship team leads the congregation into the presence of God through Spirit-filled music, dance, and creative arts that glorify the Lord.

Youth & Teens

Raising a generation of bold, fearless, and faith-driven young people who will impact their schools, cities, and nations for Christ.

Prayer & Intercession

Our intercessors stand in the gap for individuals, families, and nations. Prayer is the foundation and heartbeat of everything we do at Gilgal.

Children's Ministry

Creating a fun, safe, and Jesus-centered environment where children learn about God's love and grow in faith from their youngest years.

Missions & Outreach

Taking the Gospel beyond the church walls into our communities, streets, and nations — showing God's love through action and compassion.

Women of Dominion

Empowering and equipping women to walk in divine purpose, godly character, and kingdom influence in every sphere of life.

You are not here by accident. God has a specific plan just for you.
Leadership

Meet Our Pastor

Pst. David Mwangi
Senior Pastor & Founder — Gilgal Dominion Center

With a burning passion for souls and a prophetic voice that shakes nations, Pastor David has dedicated his life to seeing people discover their God-given identity and walk in true freedom. Ordained and anointed, he carries a mantle of healing, deliverance, and apostolic authority that transforms every service into a divine encounter.


Together with his beloved wife and family, he leads Gilgal Dominion Center with love, wisdom, and an uncompromising commitment to the Word of God. His vision: a church that is a refuge for the broken and a launching pad for champions.
